WP Appointments ships free on WordPress.org. Premium addons are optional, sold separately, and extend the free core without replacing it.

## What the free plugin includes

Everything needed to accept bookings end-to-end for a single-owner business.

- **Unlimited services and bookable entities.** Create as many services and bookable resources as you need. No cap, no upsell wall.
- **Customer booking flow.** A Gutenberg block renders the full booking experience — date picker, time slot selection, customer form, confirmation. Single-step and multi-step layouts.
- **Weekly schedule** with multiple time slots per day and per-day enable/disable.
- **Out-of-office dates** that override the weekly schedule.
- **Admin calendar** with appointment list, create/edit slideout, status changes (pending → confirmed → cancelled).
- **Customer management.** Customers are created from bookings, can optionally be given WordPress accounts, and have their booking history tracked.
- **Email notifications** on appointment creation, confirmation, cancellation, and updates — with editable templates and merge tags (`{customer_name}`, `{date}`, `{time}`, `{service}`, `{duration}`, …).
- **REST API** for every feature, usable from headless setups or integrations.
- **Translation-ready** with bundled translations for 10 locales.

The free plugin is not a demo, a trial, or a restricted version. It's designed to be the whole product for most solo operators.

## What premium addons add

Premium addons target multi-resource, multi-site, or paid-booking setups. Each addon is a separate plugin that depends on the free core.

| Addon | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Employees** | Multi-staff scheduling. Each staff member has their own calendar, working hours, and out-of-office. Customers pick a specific staff member during booking. |
| **Locations** | Multi-location support with per-location availability, addresses, and booking rules. Customers pick a location before booking. |
| **Payments** | Accept payment at booking time via WooCommerce. Supports deposits, full upfront payment, and refunds on cancellation. |
| **Custom Fields** | Add arbitrary fields to the booking form — text, checkboxes, dropdowns, file uploads — captured per appointment. |

## When to consider premium

- You have **more than one person taking appointments** → Employees.
- You operate from **more than one location** → Locations.
- You want to **take money** at booking time → Payments.
- You need to **collect extra info** beyond name/email/phone → Custom Fields.

If none of those apply, the free plugin covers the full flow.

## Where to get premium

Visit [wpappointments.com](https://wpappointments.com) for pricing and download. All premium addons are distributed as separate ZIP files and installed the same way as any other WordPress plugin — see [Installing Addons](/guides/premium/installing-addons) for the step-by-step.
