Schedules define when appointments can be booked. The global schedule sets your default opening hours. Individual bookable entities can override it.

## Global Schedule

Go to **Appointments Booking → Settings → Schedule** to configure:

- Opening and closing times per day of week
- Days off (uncheck the day)

## Per-Entity Overrides

Each bookable entity can have its own schedule that overrides the global one. Edit the entity and configure its availability section.
