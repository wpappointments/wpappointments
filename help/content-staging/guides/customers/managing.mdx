title: Managing Customers
description: Customer database and appointment history.
---

## Customer List

Go to **WP Appointments → Customers** to view all customers. The list supports search and pagination.

## Customer Details

Click a customer to see:
- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Appointment history
- Total bookings count

## Creating Customers

Customers are created automatically when they book through the booking flow. You can also create them manually from the admin.
