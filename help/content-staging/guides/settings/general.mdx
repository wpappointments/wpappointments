---
title: Settings
description: Configure WP Appointments settings.
---

All plugin settings are under **WP Appointments → Settings**.

## Tabs

| Tab | Settings |
|-----|----------|
| **General** | Business name, timezone, date/time format |
| **Appointments** | Default duration, buffer times, cancellation policy |
| **Calendar** | Calendar view defaults, week start day |
| **Schedule** | Opening hours per day of week |
| **Notifications** | Email templates and toggles |
